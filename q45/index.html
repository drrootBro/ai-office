Most relationships do not collapse in one moment.

They erode through patterns people misread: silence, resentment, intimacy drift, hidden imbalance, unclear boundaries, and delayed accountability.

What Q45 does

Q45 turns relationship confusion into structured pattern recognition. Instead of asking “Do they love me?”, it asks what is actually breaking: alignment, desire, trust, responsibility, communication, boundaries, or long-term viability.

What it is not

It is not romantic comfort talk. It is not random advice. It is a practical diagnostic framework for people who need clarity before making serious emotional decisions.
