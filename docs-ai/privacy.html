Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

AI Draft for Google Docs™ (“the Add-on”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is an AI writing assistant designed for Google Docs™. We take privacy and data protection seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, what we do not collect, how information is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or access any of the following:

  • Your Google Docs™ files or document content
  • Your Google account information
  • Your prompts or writing history
  • Your OpenAI or Google Gemini API keys
  • Analytics, logs, usage statistics, or telemetry
  • Personal information such as name, email address, or IP address

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

2. Information the Add-on Processes

The Add-on processes only the text you explicitly choose to send for AI rewriting, summarizing, translation, or generation. This processing occurs only during active use and is not stored or logged.

All AI requests are sent directly from your device to the AI provider you choose (OpenAI or Google Gemini). We do not proxy, intercept, or store these requests.

3. API Keys (BYOK – Bring Your Own Key)

Your API keys are:

  • Stored locally in your browser’s encrypted storage
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Never stored, logged, or backed up
  • Fully controlled by you and removable at any time

We cannot access, recover, or view your API keys.

4. Third-Party AI Providers

Depending on your selection, your text may be sent directly to one of the following providers:

  • Google Gemini API
  • OpenAI API

Your data is processed in accordance with their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any data. All processing is temporary and occurs only when you use the Add-on. No data is stored on our servers, and no backups or logs are created.

6. Children’s Privacy

AI Draft for Google Docs™ does not target and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since no user data is collected, no personal information of children is stored or retained.

7. Security

Although we do not store data, we ensure industry-standard security measures:

  • All external requests use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is transmitted to servers other than the AI provider selected by the user
  • No additional or unnecessary OAuth scopes are used
  • No data is ever used for advertising or profiling

The Add-on complies with Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
